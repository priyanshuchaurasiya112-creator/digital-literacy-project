# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips

* Never share OTPs, passwords, or banking details with anyone.
* Do not click on suspicious links received via SMS, email, or social media.
* Always verify the identity of unknown callers before taking action.
* Avoid installing apps from untrusted sources or links.
* Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication.
* Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions.

## UPI / Financial Safety Tips

* Never approve “collect requests” unless you are sure you are receiving money.
* Do not scan QR codes to receive money — QR codes are used to make payments, not receive them.
* Double-check the recipient’s name before sending money via UPI.
* Avoid sharing your screen during financial transactions.

## Reporting Cybercrime

* Report incidents immediately at: **https://cybercrime.gov.in**
* Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: **1930** for urgent financial fraud cases.
