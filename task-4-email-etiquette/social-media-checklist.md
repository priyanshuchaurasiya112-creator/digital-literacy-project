# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist for College Students

## Do’s

* **Think before posting** — consider how your content may be perceived by others.
* **Maintain professionalism** — remember that future employers may view your profile.
* **Respect privacy** — ask permission before sharing photos or information about others.
* **Verify information** — ensure news or facts are accurate before sharing.
* **Use privacy settings wisely** — control who can see your posts and personal details.

## Dont’s

* **Don’t share sensitive personal information** — avoid posting your address, phone number, or financial details.
* **Don’t engage in online arguments** — avoid conflicts or offensive discussions.
* **Don’t post inappropriate content** — this includes offensive language, images, or videos.
* **Don’t spread misinformation** — avoid sharing unverified or false content.
* **Don’t neglect your digital footprint** — remember that what you post online can be permanent.
