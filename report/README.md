
Task 1 Write-up:

I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva, which is an easy-to-use online design tool. The purpose of this infographic is to help students understand the importance of digital literacy in today’s academic and professional environment. My design covers key topics such as the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, maintaining a professional online presence, and proper email etiquette.

While designing the infographic, I focused on making the content visually appealing and easy to understand by using icons, headings, and concise points. I ensured that the information was clear and relevant for students who are just beginning to explore digital platforms.

One challenge I faced was selecting the right layout and balancing the amount of content without overcrowding the design. However, Canva’s templates helped me organize the information effectively. Overall, this task helped me understand how to present important information in a simple and engaging visual format.

Task 2 Write-up:

In this task, I created and updated my profiles on three important platforms: GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. GitHub is mainly used for storing and sharing coding projects, and it helps in building a strong technical portfolio. LinkedIn is a professional networking platform where I added my education details and will use it to connect with professionals, explore opportunities, and stay updated with industry trends. Kaggle is a platform for data science and machine learning, where I can practice problems and learn new skills.

The purpose of creating these profiles is to establish a professional digital presence early in my academic journey. These platforms are widely used by recruiters, internship coordinators, and the technical community to evaluate a student’s skills and interests.

Over the next four years, I plan to regularly update my GitHub with projects, improve my LinkedIn profile with achievements and certifications, and actively participate in Kaggle competitions. This will help me enhance my skills, build a strong portfolio, and prepare for future internships and job opportunities.
